Janssen Pharmaceuticals, Inc., is proud to support the American Diabetes Association® as it teams up with the Washington Redskins to help adults with type 2 diabetes manage their disease and prevent its complications.

To help you reach the American Diabetes Association®-recommended A1C goal of less than 7 percent (for most adults with diabetes), we're giving you the tools you need to help manage your blood sugar and live a healthier life.

Learn more about how you or someone you love can manage type 2 diabetes using some of the tools below.

Meet our captain.

Ziggy Hood #90

Position: Defensive Line

HT: 6’3”    Wt: 305 lbs

As a professional athlete, Ziggy Hood takes being physically fit and eating well very seriously. But it’s something he believes is important for everyone, not just football players. After supporting his aunt’s journey with type 2 diabetes, Ziggy felt inspired to help others develop their own healthy eating habits and utilize available resources that could help lower their blood sugar. That's why he’s leading the Washington Redskins’ campaign with the American Diabetes Association® to help make healthy living resources available to everyone.

The tools you need to succeed.

When given the tools, healthy living can be easy. Along with finding a treatment that works for you, diet and exercise are a large part of managing type 2 diabetes.
